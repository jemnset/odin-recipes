Banana Bread

Description

This is a really lovely banana bread recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Lightly grease a 9x5-ince loaf pan
  2. Put flour in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with a mixer in a separate bowl.
  3. Stir in eggs and bananas. Pour into the prepared loaf pan
  4. Bake in preheated oven for about 60 minutes
