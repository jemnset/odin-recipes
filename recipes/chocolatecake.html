Chocolate Cake

Description

This is my favorite chocolate cake recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1/2 cup natural unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup hot water

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350F and prepare an 8x8 pan
  2. In a medium size bowl sift the dry ingredients
  3. In a small bowl whisk the eggs with the buttermilk, oil and vanilla until smooth and emulsified
  4. Add the wet ingredients to dry then add hot water, whisking until combined. Pour into the pan
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes
