Honey Joys

Description

A classic Australian party food

Ingredients

  • 4 cups corn flakes
  • 1/3 cup of butter
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 1 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300F
  2. Add butter, sugar and honey to a saucepan and heat the mixture on medium-low until foamy
  3. Pour over the cornflakes and use a spoon to evenly coat the cornflakes
  4. Line a muffin pan with paper cups and spoon the mixture into the cups
  5. Bake for 10 minutes until golden
